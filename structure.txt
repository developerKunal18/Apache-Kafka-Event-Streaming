🧠 Why This Is Important

Traditional communication:
Service A
   │
HTTP Request
   ▼
Service B

Both services must be online at the same time.

With Kafka:
Producer
    │
    ▼
+------------+
|   Kafka    |
|   Topic    |
+------------+
    │
    ▼
Consumer

The producer and consumer are decoupled and communicate asynchronously.

Benefits:
✅ High throughput
✅ Fault tolerance
✅ Event replay
✅ Scalable consumers
✅ Real-time processing
